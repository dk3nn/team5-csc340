# Project-Name Requirements Testing
## Actors
- Dealer
- Customer

### Use Cases
#### 1. Dealer: Create dealer profile use case:
1. Dealer D1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.
2. P1 add a new vehicle with make.  
P1 exits the app.

#### 2. Customer: Create customer profile:
1. Customer C1 logs in for the first time and creates a profile.

#### 3. Customer:  View and Save Vehicles:
1. Customer C2 logs in for the first time and creates a new profile.
2. C2 views browse section and sees available vehicles V1 and V2.
3. C2 saves V1.

#### 4. Customer: Write review
1. C2 log in and goes to home page
2. C2 clicks dealers name attached to car to view dealer page.
3. C2 writes a positive review of dealer V1. C2 exits.

#### 5. Customer: Modify profile
1. C1 logs in and modifies their profile.
2. C1 views vehicle V1 and leaves the dealer a positive review.
3. C1 contacts D1 to purchase V1. C1 exits.

#### 6. Dealer:  sells the vehicle, View deals Statistics, & Modify Profile use cases
1. Provider D1 logs in and sells that vehicle to the customer.
2. D1 views sales and payments statistics.  D1 modifies his profile. D1 exits.
